This is the help page for FAQs and their answers

-How can I see countries and know information about them before booking?

Through our site you can know the famous tourist places in the countries shown in addition to historical information about them, by clicking on the word countries top such as this image

-How can I see the offers?

Offers and discounts can be viewed by clicking on the word "Offers" in the top bar, like this image Then you can choose the country you want to travel to

-How do I join the site?

By clicking on the word "join" and choosing "sign in" in the case of registration for the first time, or choosing "login" in the case of pre-registration

-How can you know about our website?

You can learn more about our website by clicking on the word "About".

-How to activate the dark mode?

Dark Mode can be activated by pressing this button.

-How can I book?

Reservations can be made by clicking on the word "offers", which is located at the top of the home page, and after that, the offers page is accessed, and then the word "book now" is clicked.
